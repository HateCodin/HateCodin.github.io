Chocolate Fizz

Ingredientes

  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 4 ice cubes, crushed

Instrucciones

  1. Combine all ingredients i a blender and blend until the ice is pulverized and the drink is thick and foamy.
  2. For a fruit frappe, freeze 1/2 cup of your favorite fresh, frozen or canned fruit and add it instead of the ice cubes.
  3. Variations: Add 1/4 teaspoon of another extract along with the vanilla, such as almond, lemon, maple, mint or rum.
  4. Serve immediately, straight up or on ice.
