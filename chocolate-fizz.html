Chocolate Fizz

Ingredientes

  • 1-2 oz. chocolate syrup
  • 6 oz. 1% milk
  • 2 oz. cold club soda

Instrucciones

  1. All ingredients should be cold.
  2. In a tall glass, stir syrup and milk until well-blended.
  3. Slowly pour the club soda down the side of the glass stirring constantly to blend.
  4. Serve immediately, straight up or on ice.
